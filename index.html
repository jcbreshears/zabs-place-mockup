What is ZABS Place?

More than a thrift store. A launchpad.

We're a nonprofit boutique in downtown Matthews where the shelves are stocked with secondhand treasures — and staffed by young adults with special needs learning to work. Every shift builds confidence, real skills, and a résumé that lands them careers with employers across Charlotte. Where others see "secondhand," we see what's possible.

The journey

From the racks to a real career.

Our Sam Ebersman Job Training Program walks every trainee from first day to first paycheck.

1

Apply & Join

A warm, low-pressure application — no résumé or job history needed.

2

3

Build Skills

Coaching on the soft skills, confidence, and independence employers want.

4

Get Hired

We connect grads with local employers ready to welcome their talents.
